📊 My Results
📕 Mistake Notebook

Every question you've gotten wrong, in one place — with the right answer, the explanation, and a tag for why you missed it. Fixing repeat mistakes is the fastest way to raise your score.

🔁 Revision

Spaced repetition resurfaces questions you missed on a 1 → 7 → 30 → 90 day schedule — right before you'd forget them. Get one right four reviews running and it's marked mastered.

🎯 Weak Areas

Your lowest-accuracy topics, ranked. Hit Practice on any of them to generate a fresh, AI-verified set aimed straight at that weakness.

📈 Insights

What your confidence and timing reveal — where you're confidently wrong, where you're guessing well, and where you're underselling what you actually know.

🧩 Coverage

How much of the syllabus you've actually practiced, and how solid each part is — covered, strong, or due for revision. The gaps show what you haven't touched yet.

🎓 Readiness

An honest "if the exam were tomorrow" picture — your strengths, what to revise last, and an estimated score range from your own practice. Never a cutoff guarantee.

🎤 Interview Prep

The board builds most of its questions from your DAF — hometown, education, optional, hobbies, work. Fill in your details and AIMENTORA predicts the question bank a real board would build for you, with a preparation hint for each question.
